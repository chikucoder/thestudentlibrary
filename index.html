About Us

The Student Library offers a peaceful and focused environment designed for study, research, and personal growth. With thousands of books, journals, and digital resources, our space is a hub for learners to explore knowledge and expand their horizons in a quiet, well-equipped setting.

Newly opened and managed by Sandesh Kumar, our library is located from coordinates (25.1292595, 83.9240243) to Punjab National Bank, 4WHG+MPM, SH17, near Police Station, Kargahar, Bihar 821107 via Kargahar Phooli Path. We proudly offer modern features such as air conditioning, individual desks with charging ports, high-speed Wi-Fi, and motivational decor — all tailored to create the perfect study experience.
